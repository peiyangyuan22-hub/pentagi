## Maintainer Responsibilities

- Review PRs within 48 hours
- Triage issues weekly
- Cut releases monthly
- Maintain CI/CD pipeline health
- Moderate community spaces (Discord, Telegram, GitHub)

## Becoming a Maintainer

Frequent contributors who demonstrate deep knowledge of the codebase and a collaborative attitude may be invited to become maintainers. The process:

1. 10+ approved PRs or significant architectural contributions
2. Nominated by an existing maintainer
3. Approved by consensus of current maintainers
4. Given direct push access to the repository

## Emeritus

Previous maintainers who are no longer active. Welcome back anytime.
